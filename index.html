Bio

During undergrad, I studied psychology and public health leading me to a fulfilling career in HIV/AIDS prevention. It was at this time I was exposed to SQL and my passion for answering abstract questions and using the answers to make important decisions intersected with my interest in technology.

I loved that the tech field allowed me to use my creativity to develop solutions and I decided to pursue the exciting opportunity to study software development with Generation USA. I am currently developing my technical skills and looking forward to the opportunity to combine my interpersonal communication skills gained from my social science background, to collaborate with others on web development projects.
